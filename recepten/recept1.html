Prep 15 min • Cook 10 min • Total 25 min • Serves 2
veggie dinner first warm day friends

Ingredients

  • 200 grice noodles
  • 1chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsptamarind paste
  • 2 tbspsoy sauce
  • 1lime, juiced
  • 2garlic cloves, minced
  • 40 gpeanuts, crushed

Method

  1. Boil noodles until just tender; reserve some cooking water.
  2. Brown the chicken in a hot pan; set aside.
  3. Combine tamarind, soy, lime and a splash of water for the sauce.
  4. Stir-fry garlic briefly, add noodles and sauce; loosen with reserved water.
  5. Return chicken, toss, finish with spring onion, coriander and peanuts.

Notes

Swap chicken for tofu to make it vegan (use soy + tamarind + maple). Add chili to taste.

Tips

  • Keep some noodle water to loosen the sauce.
  • Don’t overcook the noodles; finish them in the pan.

~520 kcal · 24 g protein · 60 g carbs · 18 g fat
